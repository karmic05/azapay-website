Your Financial Trust Score

A 300–850 score that turns your real activity into proof of creditworthiness. It recomputes continuously — and we show you exactly what's moving it.

What the score is built from

  • Repayment history35%
    On-time repayment of any prior AzaPay credit.
  • Cash-flow stability20%
    Consistent inflows and a positive net balance over time.
  • Savings behaviour15%
    Regular contributions and untouched savings balances.
  • Bill & obligation reliability15%
    On-time airtime, utilities, and rent payments.
  • Transaction depth & tenure10%
    Volume, variety, and length of platform activity.
  • Verification level5%
    KYC tier completeness and identity confidence.
▲

Positive events lift it

On-time repayments, steady savings, and consistent inflows raise your score gradually — unlocking higher limits and cheaper pricing.

▼

Setbacks lower it fairly

Missed repayments or erratic activity pull it down, with the steepest penalty on missed credit — but never with harassment.

⟲

Recent behaviour counts most

Recency is weighted heavily, so a single setback doesn't define you. Get back on track and your score recovers.

👁

Always explainable

You see the top factors moving your score and concrete actions to improve it — good UX and aligned with fair-lending expectations.

We start with a transparent rules-plus-scorecard model for the first cohorts, then layer machine learning once there's enough labelled repayment data to train and validate it responsibly.
