How we keep credit fair

Graduated limits

Limits rise only after on-time repayment, so risk grows with proven behaviour.

Auto-debit first

Transparent pricing

Every fee, interest charge, and penalty is shown as one total cost of credit before you accept.

Fair default handling

No harassment, no contact-list shaming — just structured restructuring offers and licensed bureau reporting.
