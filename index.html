The problem

Economically active. Financially invisible.

Millions of Nigerians earn, spend, and save every day — yet without formal employment records, collateral, or a credit history, banks have no reliable way to assess them. So they get excluded by default.

🚫

No credit history

Without a formal track record, traditional lenders reject thin-file applicants automatically — regardless of how reliably they actually handle money.

📄

No collateral, no documents

Gig workers, traders, and informal earners rarely have payslips or assets to pledge — the very things old underwriting depends on.

🔒

Locked out of growth

No fair credit means no working capital, no cushion for emergencies, and no path to build wealth or a financial identity over time.

What you get

One app. The full financial journey.

AzaPay combines what most providers keep separate — day-to-day payments, an identity you can build, and credit that was previously out of reach.

Digital Wallet

A secure mobile wallet to send, receive, and store money. It's your everyday entry point — and the source of the data that powers everything else.

Savings Tools

Set money aside automatically and build wealth over time. Consistent saving also feeds positively into your Trust Score.

Core differentiator

AI Underwriting Engine

Instead of relying on credit bureaus, the engine scores five alternative signals — transaction activity, savings behaviour, remittance flows, bill payments, and income patterns — so we can lend to people banks reject, while keeping risk under control.

Financial Trust Score

A dynamic score built from real behaviour rather than banking status. It updates as you transact, save, and pay — a credit profile you've never had before.

Responsible Lending

Affordable credit unlocked by your score — with terms driven by demonstrated behaviour, not collateral or formal employment. Transparent pricing, fair defaults.

Personalized Insights

Tailored guidance that helps you understand your money, improve your habits, and strengthen your Trust Score — one clear action at a time.

  • Repayment history35%
    On-time repayment of any prior AzaPay credit.
  • Cash-flow stability20%

  • Savings behaviour15%

  • Bill & obligation reliability15%

▲

Positive events lift it

On-time repayments, steady savings, and consistent inflows raise your score gradually — unlocking higher limits and cheaper pricing.

▼

Setbacks lower it fairly

Missed repayments or erratic activity pull it down, with the steepest penalty on missed credit — but never with harassment.

⟲

Recent behaviour counts most

Recency is weighted heavily, so a single setback doesn't define you. Get back on track and your score recovers.

Getting started

Verified in minutes, not weeks

Nigeria's tiered KYC — anchored on BVN and NIN — is built for exactly the people banks leave behind. Open a wallet instantly, then unlock more as you verify.

  1. 1

    Sign up with your phone

    Verified by a one-time code (OTP). No paperwork.

  2. 2

    Add your NIN or BVN

    We retrieve your identity electronically from NIMC or the NIBSS BVN database — done in minutes.

  3. 3

    Quick liveness check

    A selfie matched against your verified record confirms it's really you.

  4. 4

    Wallet opens instantly

    Start transacting on a Tier 1 wallet while higher tiers verify in the background.

  5. 5

    Unlock more

    Add a second identifier and address to reach Tier 2 & 3 — bigger payments and credit.

Built on trust

Compliant by design, private by default

Nigeria has tightened fintech and lending rules — so for us, compliance is foundational, not optional. And we built privacy in where earlier lenders failed.

CBN-aligned

We operate on top of a licensed bank or Mobile Money Operator and a payments aggregator for NIP access — the capital-light, regulated route.

FCCPC digital-lending rules

Designed to register under the DEON Consumer Lending Regulations 2025, with plain-language pricing and a fast, fair complaints process.

NDPA 2023

Built around the Nigeria Data Protection Act — with a Data Protection Impact Assessment and consent-based data sharing under CBN open banking.

We never touch your contacts

No access to your contacts, call logs, or photo gallery — ever. That rules out the contact-shaming tactics that got earlier loan apps sanctioned.
