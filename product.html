What you get

One app. The full financial journey.

AzaPay combines what most providers keep separate — day-to-day payments, an identity you can build, and credit that was previously out of reach.

Core features

Digital Wallet

A secure mobile wallet to send, receive, and store money. It's your everyday entry point — and the source of the data that powers everything else.

Savings Tools

Set money aside automatically and build wealth over time. Consistent saving also feeds positively into your Trust Score.

Core differentiator

AI Underwriting Engine

Instead of relying on credit bureaus, the engine scores five alternative signals — transaction activity, savings behaviour, remittance flows, bill payments, and income patterns — so we can lend to people banks reject, while keeping risk under control.

Financial Trust Score

A dynamic score built from real behaviour rather than banking status. It updates as you transact, save, and pay — a credit profile you've never had before.

Responsible Lending

Affordable credit unlocked by your score — with terms driven by demonstrated behaviour, not collateral or formal employment. Transparent pricing, fair defaults.

Personalized Insights

Tailored guidance that helps you understand your money, improve your habits, and strengthen your Trust Score — one clear action at a time.
